Services Offered by The Temple Inn & Suites

The Temple Inn & Suites caters to members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints who are patrons at the nearby temple of the Church. In that capacity, the Temple Inn offers the following specialized services to guests and visitors who serve in the temple:

Specialized Services

  • Free access to changing rooms & bathroom facilities
  • A playroom and low-cost, short-term babysitting services
  • Long-term stay accommodations for full-time missionary couples
  • Temple history themed reception and sitting area
  • A wedding reception hall that can be easily customized
  • A family search center that supports family search and ordinance work

We also provide the following amenities to Temple Inn guests:

Amenities

  • Breakfast
  • Exercise/workout room
  • Indoor pool
  • WiFi high-speed internet and smart TVs
  • Parking
  • Temple shuttle
  • Family history consultation and family ordinance card service
