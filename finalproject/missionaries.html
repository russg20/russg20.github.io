Long-term Accommodations for Full-Time Temple Missionaries

The Temple Inn & Suites offers a limited number of one-bedroom kitchenette suites to couples called to serve as Full-Time Temple missionaries. These suites are available to rent on a long-term basis for a reasonable monthly fee with no deposit required. See below for the features of these suites.

Features of Suites

  • Small livingroom area
  • One bathroom with full size tub/shower
  • Appliances: microwave, small stove/oven, refrigerator
  • Fully stocked kitchenette
  • Laundry room onsite
  • WiFi high-speed internet access
  • Assigned parking space

The nearby community provides all the benefits of a large city: shopping plazas, a library, gas stations, and medical facilities. The ward building is within driving distance of each of our locations.
