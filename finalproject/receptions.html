Accommodations for Wedding Receptions

The Temple Inn & Suites has a small ballroom available for use to by anyone willing to abide by the company's terms and policies. The ballroom seats 100 guests and can be customized with different layouts and catering options. Please see the options listed below for a description of reception services.

Setup Options

The Temple Inn & Suites has several options available for the layout of your wedding reception. You can mix and match them to achieve the configuration that best meets your needs. We offer the following elements for wedding receptions held in our ballroom. If there is something you desire that is not on the list, just inquire about it. We will try to make every accommodation you need.

  • Round tables - each seating eight guests
  • Rectangle tables - each seating ten guests
  • Bride and Groom table
  • Welcome table with Guest Book
  • Gift table
  • Buffet tables
  • Cake table
  • Dance floor - 15ft by 10ft
  • DJ section
  • Latticework arch

Catering Options

The Temple Inn & Suites offers several choices when it comes to food service. You can choose one of the following options.

  • Option A: Dessert Buffet, including foods like cookies, brownies, lemon bars, & muffins
  • Option B: Hors d'oeuvres Buffet, including finger sandwiches, veggie trays, cheese & cracker trays, and fruit trays
  • Option C: Dinner Buffet, including a choice of a taco bar, an Italian pasta bar, or a salad bar
  • Option D: Use of the adjoining kitchen to cook and supply your own food

Music Options

The following options are offered for supplying music for your reception.

  • We can supply the DJ for a moderate fee
  • You can use our sound system with ipod hookups
  • You can bring in your own DJ
