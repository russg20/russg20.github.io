Page Name: Weather-R-Us

Available Domain: weatherrus.info

Site Purpose:

The purpose of the Weather-R-Us website is to provide detailed forecasts including things like current, high, and low temperatures, expected precipitation, air quality index, pollen count, and the time of sunrise and sunset. We want to provide a go-to site for all the weather data a resident of the Idaho cities of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven will need to plan their day, regardless of the purpose for their need. This site will also collect storm data from local residents to aid other residents to weather the storm effectively.
