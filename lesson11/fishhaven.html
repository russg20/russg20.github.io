Upcoming Events in Fish Haven

Ring in Christmas!

by Ginger Russ

Every year at this time there is unusual activity in the park at the center of Fish Haven. Boxes of lights and decorations are strewn about the largest evergreen tree in the park as Rangers and volunteers prepare the tree for the annual tree lighting ceremony that takes place in mid-December. You might see ladders and scissor lifts nearby as the individuals in charge of this event direct the feverish activity of transforming this ordinary tree into a Christmas tree. Strands of lights need to be strung and large decorations hung in preparation for that “most wonderful time of the year.” This year the annual “Light the Tree” event will take place on Thursday, December 12 at 6:00pm. Please join us for an evening of caroling, laughter, and hot cocoa as we welcome the Christmas season with a tree lighting ceremony in the park. All are invited to attend!
